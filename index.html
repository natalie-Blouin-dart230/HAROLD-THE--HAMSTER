Harold the Astronaut Hamster has led a life of wonder and discovery, far beyond the typical hamster’s wildest dreams. Born in a cozy hamster habitat, Harold always had a curious spirit, gazing up at the night sky through his cage bars, longing for adventure. His fascination with the stars led him to a rigorous training program at the Intergalactic Hamster Academy, where he was taught the ways of space navigation, piloting, and survival in zero gravity.
